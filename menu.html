CHICKEN INASAL

Chicken Inasal is a popular Filipino dish known for its flavorful and succulent grilled chicken. It typically consists of marinated chicken pieces, often thigh and leg portions, infused with a blend of ingredients such as calamansi (Filipino lime), vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, and achuete or annatto oil, which gives it its distinctive reddish hue.

Pork Barbecue consists of succulent skewered pork slices marinated in a tantalizing blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ketchup, and spices. INIHAW NA LIEMPO

$15.00

Inihaw na Liempo is a Filipino grilled dish featuring thinly sliced pork belly marinated in a blend of soy sauce, calamansi or lime juice, garlic, and pepper. It's grilled until charred and crispy on the outside while remaining tender and flavorful inside, often served with steamed rice and a side of dipping sauce made from vinegar, soy sauce, and spices.

PORK SISIG

$14.00

Pork Sisig is a Filipino sizzling dish made from chopped pork parts, often the head and liver, seasoned with onions, chili peppers, and calamansi juice, then typically served on a hot plate. It's commonly garnished with a cracked egg on top, providing a rich and savory flavor, and often served as an appetizer or main dish with steamed rice.
